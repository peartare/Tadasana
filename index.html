Yoga Trekking: resonate with nature

The natural world is the greatest source of excitement; the greatest source of visual beauty; the greatest source of intellectual interest. It is the greatest source of so much in life that makes life worth living. David Attenborough

Mountain Tadasana is a mobile studio focused on bioenergetics. We host lessons in remote mountain areas to help resonate every gathering with the natural frequencies of the environment. Each yoga practice draws on the area's local biology to increase well-being. By absorbing the energy of the mountains, we are motivated to release our inner blockages and resonate deeply with nature.

Rotating our mobile studio among serene alpine environments allows us to benefit from each area's natural frequencies. Practicing yoga in a variety of natural areas motivates the circulation of one's own primal energies through removing the routines of urban life. By overcoming the challenges of even making it into the mountains, focus and concentration are heightened, energy is more available, and your yoga practice flourishes. Natural vibrations awaken life's vital energies.

Imagine a journey

Breathe, imagine a meadow blossoming with Columbines all around you. Deep inside this Rocky Mountain valley you find a hint of peace. You continue to gather clues, but the answer is obvious, the answer is natural.

Natural surroundings have a significant impact on the human body. In addition to exercise, outdoor activities help build a strong connection with the power of nature, natural environments increase focus and awareness, and new friends are made as confidence overcomes the fear of challenge.
